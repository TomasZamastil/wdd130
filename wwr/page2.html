Fun for the whole family

Welcome to Dry Oar River Rafting, where exciting adventures meet remarkable scenery. The view includes some of the most beautiful parts of Grand Teton National Park on the Snake River. There are several sections of the Snake River that flow through Grand Teton National Park and we will take you through all of them! Embark on an unforgettable journey with us as we guide you through the wild rapids and serene waters of some of the most stunning rivers in the world. Safety is our utmost priority, ensuring you have a thrilling and secure experience from start to finish.

At Dry Oar, we believe that our voyage should never compromise safety. Our team of greatly trained and certified guides will accompany you every step of the way, providing expert instructions and equipping you with top-of-the-line safety gear. We adhere to the strictest safety standards, ensuring that your rafting experience is worry-free so you can focus on having fun.

Prepare to be captivated by the awe-inspiring scenery of the Teton National Park. Wildlife, Mountain Ranges, lush forests, cascading waterfalls and more awaits you! Our carefully selected routes take you through some of nature's most picturesque landscapes that will leave you in awe. Don’t worry about electronics! We provide electronic water safety bags that attach to the raft. You can capture the landscape while not worrying about losing your phone.

Rafting is not just about the scenery – get ready for an adrenaline rush like no other! Feel the rush of adrenaline as you tackle swift rapids, working together with your fellow adventurers to conquer the challenges that lie ahead. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-time thrill-seeker, our expeditions offer a range of difficulty levels, catering to everyone's sense of adventure.

Our experienced guides will entertain you with commentary along the way, sharing their knowledge of the local vegetation, creatures, and the history of the Snake River. This is sure to be a perfect educational moment for any family. At Dry Oar River Rafting, we are committed to providing you with an experience that combines safety, fun, and scenery. Join us on an adventure of a lifetime as we navigate the rapids, soak in the beauty of nature, and create memories that will last a lifetime. Contact us today and let the river be your guide!
